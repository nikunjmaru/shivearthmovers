About Shiv Earth Movers

Shiv Earth Movers, a team of experts incorporated to help meeting the challenges faced by construction sector in the fields of earth works, heavy equipment, supply of aggregates.

Whether its removal of large sand dunes, the excavation of basements for a skyscraper, the construction of access roads in the desert, the hire of different kind of equipment, the timely supply of building materials on site or relocation of a whole plant to a new place we have solutions for all or your problems. We are a helping hand that is always there in projects with tough time lines and large scope of subcontracting work.

We have vast range of expertise in the fields of subcontracting, heavy construction equipment rentals and transportation as well as supply of aggregates and construction materials.Shiv earth Movers also provide Earth works, Road Works, Survey Works, Rubbish Removal.

Mission

To form long-term relationships of value and service with our clients and partners by using quality services and solutions and employing the best resources and latest techniques.

Vision

Our Vision is to make Shiv Earth Movers a state brand in the field of Earth works, heavy construction services. To achieve this goal we are constantly improving our values and services in a more customer oriented manner.

Policy

Our policy is to work with our Customer providing solutions to various situations whilst supplying ongoing support at very affordable rates in a predominantly competitive market. Collectively, the Earth Movers International, with its employees, continues on our ambitious growth plan which entails working with our customers and partners to identify opportunities and innovatively drive effective solutions without compromising on our service and quality.
